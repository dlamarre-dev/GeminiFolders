Your AI conversations are scattered across five different sites. You keep retyping the same prompts. Your best chats vanish into endless histories on Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, and Perplexity.
AI Folders is the extension you need.

AI Folders is your unified AI cockpit: organize conversations from all your AI tools into shared folders, build a reusable prompt library, and inject prompts directly into any supported AI — no copy-pasting required.

🌐 5 SUPPORTED AI SERVICES

Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, and Perplexity. Conversations from different services can share the same folder. Each chat displays its service's color and logo so you always know where it came from.

✨ PROMPT MODE

📝 Personal Prompt Library: Switch to Prompt Mode with a single click on the toggle in the header. Save your best prompts with a title and full text, then find them instantly with search and sort (newest, oldest, A-Z).
▶ One-Click Injection: No more copy-pasting. Click the play button (▶) on any saved prompt to inject it directly into any supported AI's input field.
✏️ Inline Editing: Click a prompt to expand it and edit the text directly. Changes auto-save after a short pause.
🔀 New Conversation Buttons: Launch a fresh conversation on ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, or Perplexity directly from Prompt Mode — one tap per service.
📌 Pinned Prompts: Pin your essential prompts to keep them at the top of your library, separated from the rest by a visual divider.
☁️ Prompt Sync: Enable cloud sync for your prompt library independently from your folders.
⌨️ Prompt Trigger with Live Suggestions: Type # in any AI's chat field to instantly see all your saved prompts — filtered in real time as you type. Press Space on a unique match to autocomplete, or on an exact match to inject directly. Works on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot and local LLMs.

🌟 FOLDER FEATURES

⌨️ Instant Panel Shortcut: Open the panel instantly from any tab with Ctrl+Shift+1 (or Cmd+Shift+1 on Mac).
⚡ Quick Save (Right-Click & Shortcuts): Save the current conversation with a simple right-click, or use the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+S or Cmd+Shift+S on Mac) to instantly send it to "⚡ Quick Saves" — with a visual alert if it's already saved!
☑️ Multi-Select (Bulk Actions): Select multiple conversations using checkboxes to move or delete them in one click.
📑 Tab Groups Integration: Open an entire folder in a single click as a native, color-coded Chrome Tab Group.
😃 Custom Folder Emojis: Start your folder name with an emoji (e.g., "💻 Code") to replace the default folder icon.
🖱️ Drag & Drop: Reorganize your workspace by dragging conversations between folders.
📱 Mobile Sync: A toggle generates a mirror folder in your Chrome bookmarks, perfectly reproducing your folder structure and custom sort order on your phone.
🗜️ Ultra-Efficient Compression: Automatically compresses your data (LZString) to maximize Chrome's native storage and save hundreds of conversations.
📊 Storage Tracker: A sleek visual progress bar shows your available cloud storage in real time.
📁 Custom Folders & Pins: Organize your conversations by projects, themes, or categories. Pin (📌) your favorite folders to the top.
⇅ Custom Sorting: Sort folders and conversations on the fly (Newest, Oldest, A-Z) with changes instantly reflected in your mobile sync.
🤖 Smart Detection & Search: Automatic pre-fill of your conversation's exact title and a real-time search bar.
☁️ Cloud Sync & Backup: Access your organization on all your devices and export your data as JSON.
🎨 Material 3 Design & Multilingual: Ultra-compact interface with fully custom modal dialogs, automatic dark mode, and support for 43 languages!

🏠 LOCAL LLM SUPPORT

Running a local AI model (Ollama, LM Studio, Jan, or any other)? Configure AI Folders with your local URL — supports localhost and LAN addresses (192.168.x.x, custom hostnames). Quick-save, context-menu save, and prompt injection all work on most local UIs (including Open WebUI).

🛠️ HOW DOES IT WORK?

Folder Mode:
Open a conversation on any supported site (Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, or Perplexity).
Open the panel: Ctrl+Shift+1 (or Cmd+Shift+1 on Mac).
Quick save: Ctrl+Shift+S (or Cmd+Shift+S on Mac), or right-click > "Save to AI Folders".
Via the extension: Click the icon, choose a folder, and save.
Mobile: Check 📱 in the footer to sync your folders to your phone.
Use checkboxes for bulk actions, open folders as Tab Groups (📑), drag and drop to reorganize!

Prompt Mode:
Click 📝 on the toggle button in the top right of the extension.
Click ➕ Add Prompt, enter a title and your text, and save.
To use a prompt: expand it and click ▶ — it injects directly into the active AI, ready to send.
Use the service buttons (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, Perplexity) to open a new conversation on any service.
To pin a prompt: click 📍 — it moves to the top of the list.
Prompt Trigger: Type # to instantly see all your saved prompts, or keep typing to filter in real time. Press Space on a unique match to autocomplete, or on an exact match to inject.

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
Built with privacy at its core. It only requests access to the 5 supported AI sites and your optional local LLM URL. It only reads the active tab when saving a conversation or injecting a prompt. The "Bookmarks" permission is only used if you enable mobile sync. Storage via Chrome Sync: no third-party servers, no telemetry.
