Do you use Google Gemini daily for work, coding, research, or creation, but constantly lose your important prompts in an endless history?
Gemini Folders is the extension you need.

Transform your Gemini usage into a truly structured workspace by grouping your best conversations by projects, themes, or categories. No more time wasted scrolling to find an old chat!

💡 Why is it better than simple bookmarks?
Unlike classic bookmarks that end up cluttering your browser, Gemini Folders offers a dedicated, isolated management interface overlaid directly on your Gemini window.
And for when you're on the go? The extension now includes a silent mobile "bridge": an option that creates a single mirror folder in your bookmarks, allowing you to access your conversations on your phone!

🌟 MAIN FEATURES

⚡ Quick Save (Right-Click & Shortcuts): Save the conversation with a simple right-click, or use the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+S or Cmd+Shift+S on Mac) to instantly send it to "⚡ Quick Saves"!
☑️ Bulk Actions (Multi-Select): Select multiple conversations with checkboxes to move or delete them in a single click.
📑 Tab Groups: Open an entire folder in one click within a native, colorful Chrome tab group.
😃 Custom Emojis: Start your folder name with an emoji (e.g., "💻 Code") to replace the default folder icon.
🖱️ Drag & Drop: Reorganize your workspace intuitively.
📱 Mobile Sync: Access your conversations on the go! A simple toggle generates a smart folder in your Chrome bookmarks, perfectly mirroring your structure (Pinned folders > Quick Saves > A to Z) on your smartphone.
🗜️ Ultra-efficient Compression: Automatically compresses your data (LZString) to maximize Chrome's native storage and save hundreds of chats.
📊 Storage Tracker: A visual progress bar shows your available cloud space in real time.
📁 Custom Folders & Pins: Organize your conversations and pin (📌) your favorites to the top of the list with smart sorting.
⇅ Custom Sorting: Sort your folder contents on the fly (Newest, Oldest, A to Z).
🤖 Smart Detection & Search: Auto-fills the exact title of your conversation and features an instant search bar.
☁️ Cloud Sync & Backup: Access your organization across all your computers and export your data as JSON.
🎨 "Material 3" Design & Multilingual: Ultra-compact interface (with a newly redesigned footer) that adapts to dark mode and 6 languages.

🛠️ HOW DOES IT WORK?

Open an important conversation on gemini.google.com.
Express save: Use Ctrl+Shift+S, or right-click on the page > "Save to Gemini Folders".
Via the extension: Click the icon, choose a folder, and save!
For Mobile: Check the 📱 icon in the footer to send your folders to your phone.
Check the boxes for bulk actions, open your folders in tabs (📑), and use drag and drop to reorganize!

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
Built with privacy at its core. It only requests access to Gemini and only reads the active tab when saving. It requires "Bookmarks" permission solely to generate your mobile sync folder (if enabled). Localized storage via Chrome Sync: no third-party servers, no telemetry.
