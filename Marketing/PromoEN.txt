Do you use Google Gemini daily for work, coding, research, or creation, but constantly lose your important prompts in an endless history?
Gemini Folders is the extension you need.

Transform your use of Gemini into a true structured workspace by grouping your best conversations by projects, themes, or categories. No more time wasted scrolling to find an old chat!

💡 Why is it better than simple bookmarks?
Unlike classic bookmarks that end up cluttering your browser, Gemini Folders offers you a dedicated, isolated management interface overlaid directly on your Gemini window.
And for when you're on the go? The extension now includes a silent mobile "bridge": an option that creates a single mirror folder in your bookmarks to let you access your conversations on your phone!

🌟 MAIN FEATURES
⚡ Quick Save (Right Click & Shortcuts): Save the conversation via a simple right-click, or use the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+S or Cmd+Shift+S on Mac) to send it instantly to "⚡ Quick Saves"!
☑️ Bulk Actions (Multi-Select): Select multiple conversations with checkboxes to move or delete them in a single click.
📑 Tab Groups: Open an entire folder in one click in a native, colorful Chrome tab group.
😃 Custom Emojis: Start your folder name with an emoji (e.g., "💻 Code") to replace the default folder icon.
🖱️ Drag & Drop: Reorganize your workspace intuitively.
📱 Mobile Sync: Access your conversations on the go! A simple switch generates a smart folder in your Chrome bookmarks, perfectly replicating your folder tree and custom sort order on your smartphone.
🗜️ Ultra-efficient Compression: Automatically compresses your data (LZString) to maximize Chrome's native storage and save hundreds of chats.
📊 Storage Tracker: A visual progress bar shows your available cloud space in real-time.
📁 Custom Folders & Pins: Categorize your conversations and pin (📌) your favorites to the top of the list with smart sorting.
⇅ Custom Sorting: Sort your folders and conversations on the fly (Newest, Oldest, A to Z) with direct reflection on the mobile sync.
🤖 Smart Detection & Search: Automatic pre-filling of your exact conversation title and instant search bar.
☁️ Cloud Sync & Backup: Find your organization across all your computers and export your data in JSON.
🎨 "Material 3" Design & Multilingual: Ultra-compact interface with all-new custom dialog boxes, which adapts perfectly to dark mode and now supports 16 global languages!

🛠️ HOW IT WORKS?
Open an important conversation on gemini.google.com.
Express save: Use Ctrl+Shift+S, or right-click on the page > "Save to Gemini Folders".
Via the extension: Click on the icon, choose a folder, and save!
For Mobile: Check the 📱 icon in the footer to send your folders to your phone.
Check the boxes for bulk actions, open your folders in tabs (📑), and use drag and drop to reorganize!

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
Designed with privacy at the heart of its operation. It only requests access to Gemini and only reads the active tab at the moment of saving. It requires the "Bookmarks" permission only to generate your mobile sync folder (if you enable it). Localized storage via Chrome Sync: no third-party servers, no telemetry.
