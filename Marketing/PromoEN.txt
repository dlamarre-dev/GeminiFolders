Do you use Google Gemini daily for work, coding, research, or content creation, but constantly lose your important prompts in an endless history?
Gemini Folders is the extension you need.

Transform your Gemini usage into a truly structured workspace by grouping your best conversations into projects, themes, or categories.
No more wasting time scrolling to find an old chat!

💡 Why is it better than simple bookmarks?
Unlike classic bookmarks that eventually clutter your browser and mix with your daily websites, Gemini Folders offers a dedicated, isolated workspace.
Say goodbye to tedious navigation in the bookmark manager: you get a custom menu, lightning-fast search exclusive to your chats, and quick management tools—all overlaid directly on your Gemini window without ever leaving it.

🌟 MAIN FEATURES

⚡ Quick Save (Context Menu & Shortcuts): Save the active conversation directly to the folder of your choice via a simple right-click on the page, or use the global keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+Shift+S or Cmd+Shift+S on Mac) to instantly send it to a "⚡ Quick Saves" folder!
☑️ Bulk Actions (Multi-Select): Select multiple conversations at once using checkboxes to move or delete them in batches, saving you tons of clicks.
📑 Tab Groups Integration: Open an entire folder of conversations with a single click. They will automatically open in a native, color-coded Chrome Tab Group for ultimate project management.
😃 Custom Folder Emojis: Start your folder name with an emoji (e.g., "💻 Code" or "🌍 Travel") and the extension will automatically use it as the folder's icon instead of the default one.
🖱️ Drag & Drop: Intuitively reorganize your workspace by dragging and dropping your conversations between folders.
🗜️ Ultra-Efficient Compression: Automatically compresses your synced data using LZString, maximizing Chrome's native storage capacity so you can save hundreds of conversations securely.
📊 Storage Tracker: A sleek visual progress bar keeps you informed of your available cloud storage capacity in real-time.
📁 Custom Folders & Pins: Organize your conversations by themes. Pin (📌) your favorite folders to keep them always at the top of the list.
⇅ Custom Sorting: Sort your folder content on the fly (Newest, Oldest, A to Z).
🤖 Smart Detection: The extension automatically reads the page to pre-fill the exact title of your conversation. No more manual typing!
🔍 Instant Search: Find any conversation in a fraction of a second with the real-time search bar.
☁️ Cloud Sync & Backup: Access your organization across all computers connected to your Google account, and download backups in JSON format.
🎨 "Material 3" Design & Multilingual: Enjoy an ultra-compact, fluid interface that automatically adapts to Dark Mode and your browser's language.

🛠️ HOW DOES IT WORK?

Open an important conversation on gemini.google.com.
Express Save: Press Ctrl+Shift+S to quick-save, or right-click anywhere on the page and hover over "Save to Gemini Folders".
Via the Extension: Click the extension icon. The title is automatically detected. Expand the add panel (➕), choose a folder, and save!
Use checkboxes for bulk actions, open folders as Tab Groups (📑), or use drag-and-drop to reorganize!

🔒 PRIVACY & SECURITY
Built with privacy at its core. It only requests access to the Gemini domain and reads the content of the active tab at the exact moment you save. Your folders are securely stored in Chrome Sync: no third-party servers are used.
